# ✨ results

This repository is the **public results-atlas surface** of the **Panta Rhei framework**.

It mirrors the **Results** lane of the Atlas.

## What this lane does

The Results lane is a faceted discovery surface for:

- key claims
- problems addressed
- major resolutions
- result statements linked back to canonical parts and topic projections

It is **not** a hierarchy in the same way as Books or Topics.

## What lives here

- public result pages
- generated wiki mirrors for results
- result-oriented summaries and structured discovery content

## How results are meant to work

Each result bridges:

- the **canonical build** where it is derived
- the **topic projection** where it becomes intelligible in orthodox terms
